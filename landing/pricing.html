Questions you probably have

What happens after the trial?
Your account stays active. If you want to keep using HOABot, it's $19.95/month via Stripe. If not, your data sits there until you're ready — or you can export and leave.
Is there a per-unit fee?
No. One price. Whether you manage 10 units or 500, it's the same $19.95. We're not going to nickel-and-dime a volunteer board treasurer.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account settings. No phone calls, no retention specialists, no guilt. You're an adult who volunteers to run a neighborhood — we respect that.
Why so cheap?
Because HOA boards are run by volunteers who already do too much for free. This tool should cost less than a bad pizza. We'd rather have 1,000 happy boards than 10 enterprise contracts.
